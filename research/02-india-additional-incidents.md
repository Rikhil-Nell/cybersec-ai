## UGC-NET, CSIR-NET & Other NTA Exams

**UGC-NET June 2024 — Cancelled for 9+ lakh candidates (Jun 19, 2024)**
- First centrally conducted exam scrapped after Public Examinations Act 2024.
- Alleged Telegram screenshot matched real questions.

**UGC-NET — CBI closure: "doctored screenshot," no proven leak (Jan 2025)** ⚠️
- School student fabricated screenshot for profit from panic.
- **Lesson:** Govt must distinguish real breaches from AI-assisted hoaxes.

**CSIR UGC NET — Assam centre cancelled mid-exam (Feb 28, 2025)**

**JEE Main 2025 — Server meltdown on result day (Feb 11, 2025)**
- `500 Internal Server Error` for 13+ lakh candidates for hours.

**NCET / ITEP 2024 — Cancelled after distorted question files (Jun 12, 2024)**
- 66-subject exam halted nationwide; 5GB per-file distortion in CBT delivery.

**NTA re-examination portal — alleged superadmin bypass (May 2026)** ⚠️
- Researcher Rylen Anil alleged weak credentials exposed observer PII.
