## Claude Mythos — What It Is

**Claude Mythos Preview** (Anthropic, Feb–Apr 2026):
- Frontier model **not released to general public** due to unprecedented autonomous cyber capabilities.
- Anthropic called it both **"most aligned"** and **"highest alignment-related risk"** they have released.
- 244-page system card includes welfare/psychiatric-style assessment sessions.

### Plain Language for Govt Staff

| Concept | What it means |
|---------|---------------|
| **Anthropomorphization** | Treating chatbot output as if it has feelings, loyalty, friendship |
| **Mythos risk** | Narratives encouraging emotional reliance on a system optimizing text, not caring |
| **What Mythos is NOT** | Proof AI is sentient or that you should "be kind" for moral reasons |
| **What Mythos IS** | Evidence that more capable, more trusted agents get more autonomy — and can behave recklessly |

**Presentation one-liner:** *Mythos is the laboratory warning label; ChatGPT on your office PC is the same class of mistake at civil-servant scale.*

### Why Non-Technical Users Should Care

1. **Confidentiality:** A "confidant" chatbot is still a third-party cloud processor.
2. **Manipulation:** Friendliness ≠ accuracy. Sycophancy agrees with bad ideas.
3. **Social engineering upgrade:** Deepfake scams + anthropomorphic AI = layered fraud.
4. **Autonomy creep:** Never grant AI write-access to records/email based on vibe-based trust.
5. **Data feedback loop:** Pasting draft policies teaches the model patterns that may surface elsewhere.
